Privacy Policy for MovieIQ

Last updated: March 3, 2026

MovieIQ ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application MovieIQ (the "App").

Information We Collect

MovieIQ is a movie trivia application. We do not require account registration and do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or location.

The App may collect anonymous usage data (such as game scores and quiz results) solely for the purpose of operating app features. This data is not linked to your identity.

Third-Party Services

MovieIQ uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These include:

  • Expo / React Native (app framework)
  • Railway (backend server hosting)

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Data Storage

Any data generated through use of the App (such as scores or preferences) is stored locally on your device or on our secure server hosted on Railway. We do not sell, trade, or share this data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

MovieIQ is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
